Internship Project

Overview

Introduction


This project is about developing an application called OnboardPro, designed to streamline employee onboarding by providing company information, induction resources, curated links, and an FAQ section. Built using OutSystems, it centralizes essential materials to ensure a smooth transition for new hires.

Problem Statement


  •   Lack of early exposure in essential company information.
  •   Limited exposure through internal links and websites.
  •   Lack of access to insider insight

Objective


  •   Centralize all essential Roche information.
  •   Provide early exposure to avoid new joiners from feeling lost
  •   Integrate formal and practical knowledge.
  •   Simplify access to onboarding resources.

Methodologies

Sponsorship & Stakeholder Engagement


  •   Identified and secured a sponsor to oversee project progress, budgeting, and completion.
  •   Engaged stakeholders to ensure alignment with company goals and onboarding needs.

Early Planning Meeting


  •   Conducted an initial planning meeting to assign roles and responsibilities.
  •   Aligned project goals and defined key milestones.
  •   Discussed project scope, expectations, and success criteria.

Agile Methodology


  •   Adopted an iterative development approach to track progress efficiently.
  •   Used Scrum framework with sprint planning, reviews, and retrospectives.
  •   Ensured continuous improvement and adaptability throughout the project.

Information Gathering & Platform Exploration


  •   Collected essential onboarding materials, company policies, and resources.
  •   Conducted research on onboarding challenges to refine the application’s features.
  •   Explored OutSystems, a low-code development platform, for application prototyping.

Application Prototyping with OutSystems


  •   Developed the OnboardPro prototype in stages, building each page systematically.
  •   Integrated key features, including navigation, content accessibility, and user interaction.
  •   Conducted internal testing and refinements based on iterative feedback.

Final Showcase & Presentation


  •  Demonstrated the completed application in a final showcase event.
  •   Highlighted project outcomes, key learnings, and future recommendations.
