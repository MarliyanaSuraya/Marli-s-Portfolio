Flutter Project

Overview

Introduction


CampusHelp is a streamlined app for UPM students to lodge complaints, request information and improve communication.

Problem Statement


  •   Lack of an efficient system for students to address complaints and seek help.
  •   Students struggle to resolve issues due to unclear points of contact.
  •   Resolving issues takes more time than necessary.

Objective


  •   To lodge college campus grievance
  •   Provide information related to campus.
  •   Improves the settlement process and the overall students experience by fostering
          effective communication.
  •   To receive information and express concerns more effectively.

Features

Login Page

•   Displays UPM logo for familiarity and association with the university.
•   Includes a form for students to enter their username and password.
•   Provides a login button to access the application.

Home Page

•   Contains three buttons for different categories

    ◦   File a Complaint:   students to submit a complaint.
    ◦   My Complaint:   Displays previously lodged complaints.
    ◦   Retrieve Information:   Provides access to relevant information.

Lodge Complaint Page

•   Displays a text field labeled "Enter your complaint here".
•   Includes a submit button to lodge the complaint.

Lodge Complaint Page

•   Includes a button for students to select the date of the complaint.
•   When the button is pressed, a calendar pop-up appears for students to
      pick a date.

Lodge Complaint Page

•   Displays a pop-up confirmation upon complaint submission.
•   Shows the text of the complaint and the selected date.

View Complaint Page

•   Lists all previously submitted complaints.
•   Allows users to delete any complaint they wish.

Contact Page

•   Displays a list of administrators, wardens, or higher-ups.
•   Includes their personal information:
    ◦   Name
    ◦   Position
    ◦   Email
    ◦   Phone number
    ◦   Available hours
